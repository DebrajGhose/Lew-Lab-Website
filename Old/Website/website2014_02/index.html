Welcome to the Lew Lab!



We work with the tractable budding yeast, Saccharomyces cerevisiae, to study the generation, maintenance, and directionality of cell polarity. We also study the relationship between the cell cycle and cell polarity and cell shape. Check out our Research page to learn more!
